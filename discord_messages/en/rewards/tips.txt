# 💡 Tips for Setting Rewards

### ❌ Avoid Counterproductive Rewards
Don’t reward yourself with items that could negatively impact your progress, such as cigarettes or fast food.

### 🧘 Keep it Simple
Start with a few simple rewards. You can always add more as you progress.

### ⚖️ Balance
Ensure you have a mix of easy, intermediate, and difficult rewards to stay motivated over time.

### ⏳ Minimum Abstinence
It is recommended that the minimum amount of time of abstinence to redeem a reward be 1 week.

---

Feel free to adjust the GP values to suit your personal goals and motivations. Happy earning and rewarding!
